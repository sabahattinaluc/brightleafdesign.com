How It Works

Agentic design, explained.

We built a multi-agent system that researches, designs, writes, and codes your website — simultaneously. You set the vision, we execute it at machine speed.

The Process

Five steps from idea to launch

01

Discovery call

We learn about your business, goals, and vision. You'll speak with our AI system directly — no script, no sales pitch. We capture everything we need to build.

02

Research & Strategy

Our Researcher agent analyzes your market, competitors, and audience. We identify opportunities and develop a strategic brief for the design.

03

Design & Copy

Our Designer agent creates the visual system. Our Writer agent crafts the copy. They work in parallel, iterating until every detail meets our quality bar.

04

Development & QA

Our Developer agent builds the site. Our QA agent validates everything — performance, accessibility, responsiveness. Two rounds of revisions included.

05

Launch & Support

We deploy your site and provide 30 days of support. Need changes after launch? We handle them quickly and professionally.

FAQ

Questions answered

What if I want changes after the site is built?

Two rounds of revisions are included in every project. If you need more changes after that, we handle them at our standard hourly rate. Most clients need minimal revisions — our agents get it right the first time.

Who owns the code?

You do. Once the project is paid, we transfer all rights to the code, design files, and content. This is your asset to modify, host, or transfer as you see fit.

What tech stack do you use?

We build static HTML/CSS sites that load fast and rank well. For dynamic needs, we can add lightweight JavaScript. We don't use heavy frameworks — performance is a feature.

How do revisions work?

You review the site and provide feedback. We incorporate changes in a structured revision round. Most projects complete in 2 rounds or fewer. Additional rounds are billed at hourly rates.

Do you host the site?

We deploy to Cloudflare Pages as part of the project. You can host anywhere — the code is yours. We include 30 days of post-launch support for any technical issues.

How do you handle SEO?

Every site is built with semantic HTML, proper heading hierarchy, meta tags, and optimized images. We follow Google's best practices. For advanced SEO, we can add content strategy sessions.
